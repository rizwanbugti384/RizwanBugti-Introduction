About Me

I am a passionate web developer from Dera Bugti, Pakistan, with a strong foundation in modern web technologies. My educational journey began in Karachi and continued in Quetta, where I completed my education from Grade 5 to Matriculation at FGEI Quetta School. I have recently taken admission to complete my Intermediate in ICS at Tameer-e-Nau College.

I am a certified graduate of the Saylani Mass IT Training (SMIT) program with professional skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I specialize in building clean, functional, and responsive websites that provide an excellent user experience.

Looking ahead, I plan to pursue a BS degree and am dedicated to advancing my career on an international stage. My goal is to join a global team and continue learning — with hopes to work in America, InshaAllah.
